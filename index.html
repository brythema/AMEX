Our Story

Bespoke Footwear,
Handcrafted with Integrity

AMEX Footwear was born from a simple belief: that the shoes you wear should tell a story of quality, heritage, and personal style.

Founded in Abuja, Nigeria, we set out to create footwear that rivals the best in the world — using only the finest original leather and skin, sourced responsibly and crafted with care. Every pair of AMEX shoes is handcrafted by skilled artisans who pour decades of experience into each stitch, each cut, each finish.

We believe in longevity. In a world of fast fashion, we choose to make shoes that last — that become more comfortable with time, that develop a patina unique to the wearer, that stand as a testament to the art of shoemaking.

We don't just make shoes. We make heirlooms.

  • Built for Longevity
